Margherita Pizza

Easy

A classic Italian pizza known for its simplicity and fresh flavors. This pizza uses basic ingredients but delivers a rich and satisfying taste.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Fresh tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the base
  3. Add mozzarella cheese generously
  4. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden
  5. Garnish with basil and drizzle olive oil

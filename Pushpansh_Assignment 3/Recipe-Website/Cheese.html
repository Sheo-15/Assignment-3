Cheese Pizza

Hard

A crisp-to-tender dough base, savory tomato sauce, and melted, bubbly mozzarella cheese, often with a hint of dried oregano.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Processed Cheese
  • Toppings
  • Olive oil

Instructions:


Spread pizza topping on pizza base, sprinkle cheese and top it up with onion, tomato, capsicum and sweet corn. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until cheese melts & pizza base turns light brown in colour. Season with chilli flakes & oregano. Cut into 4 pieces and serve hot.

Pepperoni Pizza

Medium

A classic, popular pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and slices of pepperoni, which is an American-style spicy, cured sausage made from beef and pork.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Shredded Mozarella Cheese
  • Thinly Sliced Pepperoni
  • Toppings

Instructions:


To make a classic pepperoni pizza, stretch 12-inch pizza dough and spread it with tomato sauce, mozzarella, and pepperoni slices. Bake in a preheated high-temperature oven at 450-500°F (230-260°C) for 8–10 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
